Our Goal

We aim to create a global event that pushes childrens ideas and solutions regarding the environment into the limelight. We believe children today, especially children from more rural areas of the world, have their voices silenced by adults, no matter how engenius thier ideas might be. This is unacceptable.

Children's Environment Day will empower the children's outputs by publishing their soltions to our webpage, online newspapers, and physical newspapers. The kids will also have the oppurtunity to present their solutions live on streaming services such as Twitch and YouTube. In rural areas with difficulties surronding internet connections, we will offer to set up temporary stations for publication and education.

The Contest

We will also run a tournament-style contest during the event. In this contest the goal is to have the best idea or solution regarding an environmental issue. Any of the attending children can be a contestant, and the contestants compete by presenting their idea in front of jury and/or an audience. The best in the locals will move on to nationals and then internationals and finally to the finals.

The winner and runner-ups will all recieve an oppurtunity to make their idea a reality.
